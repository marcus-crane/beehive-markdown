Convenor, Ministerial Group on Climate Change, Pete Hodgson, departs tomorrow to attend the 10th meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change that is being held in Buenos Aires.

Mr Hodgson will return to New Zealand on Sunday, 19 December.
