Hon Judith Tizard, Associate Minister for Arts, Culture and Heritage, today announced the re-appointment of Diana Fenwick to the Board of the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra (NZSO).

“I have re-appointed Diana Fenwick for a further three-year term on the NZSO Board”, Ms Tizard said. “Her strong commitment to the arts and her governance expertise will continue to strengthen the Board in advancing the Orchestra’s goals”.

Diana Fenwick is a trustee of the Aotea Centre Performing Arts Trust and the NZSO Foundation Trust in addition to her membership of the NZSO Board.

Judith Tizard expressed her confidence in the re-appointment. “I am delighted that Ms Fenwick has agreed to continue to serve on the Board of the NZSO. She brings an extremely impressive range of skills and experience to the Board and I am confident that the Symphony Orchestra will be continue to be well served by her participation and contribution”.
