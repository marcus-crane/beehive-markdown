Attorney-General Margaret Wilson today announced the appointment of Helen Diana Winkelmann as a Judge of the High Court.

Justice Winkelmann graduated from Auckland University and for a number of years was a partner with Phillips Fox. She started practice as a barrister sole in 2001.

Justice Winkelmann specialises in commercial litigation, insolvency law and medico legal law. As part of that specialist experience she has delivered a number of Auckland District Law Society seminars on aspects of company law including creditors remedies and fiduciary duties.

Outside of the law Justice Winkelmann has been a trustee of the Starship Foundation and the Lynfield College Development Trust.

The new judge will sit in Auckland.
