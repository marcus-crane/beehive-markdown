Associate Arts, Culture and Heritage Minister Judith Tizard has announced the re-appointment of the Chair of the National War Memorial Advisory Council, Colonel Andrew Renton-Green, for a further three-year term expiring on 31 July 2007.

Colonel Renton-Green has extensive army experience in military intelligence and security. His organisational and leadership skills have proven valuable on the National War Memorial Advisory Council, particularly in his abilities in project management.

Judith Tizard expressed her confidence in this reappointment. “I am delighted that Colonel Renton-Green has agreed to continue to chair the National War Memorial Advisory Council. He has brought an impressive range of skills and experience in project management to the Council and I am confident that the Council will continue to be well served with his hand on the helm.”

The National War Memorial Advisory Council advises the Ministers for Arts, Culture and Heritage on all matters relevant to the control and maintenance of the [National War Memorial](http://www.nationalwarmemorial.govt.nz/index.html).
