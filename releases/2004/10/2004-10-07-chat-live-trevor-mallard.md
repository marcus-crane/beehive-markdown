This is a great way to have your say, get your questions answered and let us know what you really think.

Tell us what your friends and workmates have got to say about some very topical issues - powhiri at school functions, standards in education, and more!

You can also text your chat for FREE to LABR (5227). Text CHAT then leave a space then write your comment (maximum of 150 words).

**Where:**www.headsup.co.nz/chat.
