Commerce Minister Margaret Wilson today released the [terms of reference](http://www.med.govt.nz/buslt/bus_pol/bus_law/financial-markets/security-review/task-force/tor/index.html) for the [Task Force](http://www.med.govt.nz/buslt/bus_pol/bus_law/financial-markets/security-review/task-force/index.html) on the Regulation of Financial Intermediaries.

The task force was [announced](http://www.beehive.govt.nz/?q=node%2F20732) last week, to help raise the standard of financial services and examine regulation of financial advisers. It is a part of the government’s overall objective to [enhance the integrity and effectiveness of New Zealand’s capital markets](http://www.med.govt.nz/buslt/bus_pol/bus_law/financial-markets/security-review/index.html).

Margaret Wilson is writing to industry stakeholders to request expressions of interest in the task force but welcomes applications from all those with broad experience in the financial services industry. The six-member task force should be appointed by mid-October.

Expressions of interest, together with brief curriculum vitae detailing relevant qualifications and experience, should be received by 5:00pm on Friday 17 September and be sent to:
