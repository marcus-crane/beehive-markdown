Pete Hodgson, Minister of Energy, Transport, and Research Science and Technology, will this week attend a series of OECD and bi-lateral intergovernmental meetings with French Ministers in Paris.

The meetings cover a range of topics under the sustainable development umbrella with a focus on transport and energy.

Pete Hodgson will also call in on the New Zealand Paralympic team in Athens.
