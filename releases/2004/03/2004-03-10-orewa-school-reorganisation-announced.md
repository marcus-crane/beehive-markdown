A school reorganisation for three Orewa Schools was announced by Education Minister Trevor Mallard today.

From the beginning of 2005 Orewa College will become a Year 7 to 13 (Form 1 to 7) school, and Orewa Primary and Orewa North Schools will no longer be able to accept Year 7 and 8 children for enrolment.

The decision arises from the Hibiscus Coast Schooling Strategy, a complex plan developed through extensive consultation with the community and schools since a working party was first established in 1998.

"I am pleased that the implementation of this important part of the strategy can be given the official go-ahead. It is important for the schools involved to know so that they can plan staffing and resourcing for 2005, and for parents to know which school will enrol their children."

The decision will affect those children who will be in Year 7 and 8 from 2005 onwards and who would have otherwise attended Orewa Primary or Orewa North Schools or who are resident in Red Beach. Their schooling will in future be provided at Orewa College.
