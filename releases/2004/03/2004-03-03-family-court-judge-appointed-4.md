Attorney-General Margaret Wilson today announced the appointment of Robert Murfitt of Christchurch as a Family Court Judge. He will be sworn in on 10 March and begin sitting in New Plymouth from 19 April.

Robert Murfitt, 55, gained an LLB (Hons) from the University of Canterbury in 1971 and was admitted to the bar the following year. Initially employed by Weston Ward & Lascelles, he then practised in partnerships until establishing a practice as a barrister sole in 1996.
