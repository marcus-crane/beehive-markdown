Of the 74 new constables graduating today from Wing 216 at the Porirua Police College, 61 are bound for the North Island and 13 are headed for police districts in the South Island.

Police Minister George Hawkins says of those posted to the North Island, 40 will go to the three Auckland policing districts, nine to other North Island Districts and 12 to the Wellington District.
