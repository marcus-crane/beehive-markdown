**06:25**  
Depart Auckland for Christchurch.

**08:30**  
PM opens the National Sister Cities conference at the Christchurch Convention Centre.

**10:15**  
Cashmere High School - opening of a conductive education unit.

**11:40**  
Radio call with Michael Gordon, Melbourne Age.

**12:00**  
Lincoln University and the Board of the National Centre for Bio-Protection Technologies - launch National Centre for Bio-Protection Technologies.

**12:20**  
Visit to office and laboratory facilities, meet heads of partner institutions. Unveiling of plaque.

**14:00**  
Christchurch Girls' High School - opening of the new art block.

**16:30**  
Depart Christchurch for Auckland.

**18:45**  
Holmes Show at TVNZ.

**19:30**  
YWCA Auckland - Leading Women fundraising dinner.

**22:00**  
Depart for home.
