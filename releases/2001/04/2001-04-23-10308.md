ADVISORY

Tuesday 24 April  
The first meeting of the Manufacturing Industry Sector Steering Group is to be held tomorrow 24 April 2001.

The meeting has been convened by Deputy Prime Minister Jim Anderton bringing together relevant government agencies ('whole of government' approach) with industry sector representatives. The aim is to identify barriers to the industry creating jobs and building economic activity and work to remove these barriers.

This is the second industry steering group and follows the successful work to date of the Wood Processing Industry Steering Group.
