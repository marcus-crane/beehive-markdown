Health Minister Annette King received the report on the cervical screening inquiry at 8am today.

"I am pleased to have received this long-awaited report today and will read it thoroughly over the weekend," Mrs King said.

In the meantime, the report will be going to the printers. It will be publicly released in Gisborne on Tuesday, 10 April, as previously advised.
