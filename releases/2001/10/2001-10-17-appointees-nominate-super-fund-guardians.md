, 17 October 2001 Media Statement  
Appointees to nominate Super Fund guardians

Finance Minister Michael Cullen today announced the membership  
of an independent committee to nominate people for appointment to the Board of Guardians for the New Zealand Superannuation Fund.

Under the New Zealand Superannuation legislation recently passed by  
Parliament, the Minister can only appoint Board members if they have first been nominated by this independent committee.

"I am extremely pleased at the calibre of these appointments and look forward to receiving their recommendations for the Board in two to three months," Dr Cullen said.

Those appointed to the committee are:

Vance Arkinstall (Chairman). Mr Arkinstall is the Chief Executive of  
the Investment Savings and Insurance Association (ISI).

Bruce Abraham. Mr Abraham is the former Chief Executive of Colonial  
First State Investment Managers (NZ) Ltd and current chairman of  
Colonial First State Property Trust Ltd.

Sid Ashton O.N.Z.M. Mr Ashton is the Chief Executive Officer of Te Runanga o  
Ngai Tahu. He is a director of Ryman Healthcare Ltd and a chartered  
accountant.

Annette Lusk. Ms Lusk is an Executive Director of New Zealand  
Businesses for Social Responsibility.

Rodger Spiller. Dr Spiller is the Managing Director of Money Matters  
(NZ) Ltd. He is a serving member of the Securities Commission and a  
director of New Zealand Businesses for Social Responsibility.
