What:Media briefing on central/local government forum  
Time:12.45 pm  
Date:14 December 2001  
Where:Premier House, Wellington

Background

The fourth Central/Local Government Forum is being held in Premier House in Wellington tomorrow.  
The Forum is held bi-annually and is co-chaired by the Prime Minster and the President of Local Government New Zealand.  
Jim Anderton, Acting Prime Minister, will be chairing this Forum with Margaret Shields, Acting LGNZ President.  
Issues on the agenda for this Forum include:  
·the new Local Government Bill and Rating Bills,  
·utilities management,  
·sustainable development,  
·compliance costs,  
·environment and emergency management policy.

There will be a media briefing following the Forum at 12.45pm for 15 minutes at Premier House.  
The papers from the Forum will be made available to the media at the  
media briefing.
