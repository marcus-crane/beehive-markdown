7 August 2001Media Statement  
Roading Meeting with Far North Council

The Minister of Transport today confirmed that he had a very valuable meeting with Far North District Councillors Joe Carr, Johnson Davis and Robert Lowe last Friday, where they outlined the major transport issues facing the region.

Mr Gosche said he was currently in discussions with his colleagues about these matters.

"Northland's needs are being considered as part of the government's overall approach to future transport policy."

For more information contact Lyn Holland in Mark Gosche's office, 04 4719 063, mobile 025 249 0461.
