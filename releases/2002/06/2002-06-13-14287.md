13 June 2002For Immediate ReleaseMedia Statement  
Pre-Election Economic and Fiscal Update

The Pre-Election Economic and Fiscal Update is being released at 2.00pm on Tuesday 25 June 2002.  
Under the requirements of the Fiscal Responsibility Act, Treasury is required to produce an economic and fiscal update prior to the election.  
Details on how the update will be released will be advised closer to the time.
