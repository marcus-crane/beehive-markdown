General explanation of costing of latest offer compared to May 2002 settlement.

·The cost of the May 2002 settlement over the three fiscal years 2001/02 to 2003/04 was $150m.

·This included the proposed third tranche increase of 2% with effect from July 2003. The cost of the third tranche was approximately $20m in 2003/04.

·The cost of the May 2002 settlement excluding the cost of the third tranche was $130m over the 2001/02 to 2003/04 period.

·There is no third tranche proposed as part of the Government’s latest offer which is for a shorter agreement that that reached in May 2002.i.e expire 30 April 2003 in this offer, expire 30 April 2004 in May settlement.

·The cost of the latest government offer including the NCEA Implementation Units is $143m over the period 2001/02 to 2003/04 – reflecting additional funding in this period of $13m.
