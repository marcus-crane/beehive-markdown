Governor-General Dame Silvia Cartwright and Prime Minister Helen Clark will visit Waitangi on February 5 and 6.

On 5 February they will attend a powhiri on the Waitangi National Marae. On Waitangi Day the official party will attend events beginning with a dawn karakia on the National Marae. The Prime Minister will host a breakfast for local Northland leaders before leaving Waitangi for other Waitangi Day events elsewhere. Further details will be issued later.

The Prime Minister thanked Te Tai Tokerau MP and Under-Secretary Dover Samuels, the Tai Tokerau District Maori Council and the Waitangi Day Organising Committee for their work in making the visit possible.

Tuesday 5 February

> 4.00pm Powhiri on National Marae

Wednesday 6 February

> 5.00am Dawn karakia on National Marae  
> 7.30am Breakfast for Northland leaders  
> 10.00am Official party departs
