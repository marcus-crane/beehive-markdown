Associate Minister of Justice Hon Margaret Wilson said this morning she has talked again with the Race Relations Commissioner Gregory Fortuin.

“We have agreed to reflect on the situation,” she said, “and to meet after Easter.”

Margaret Wilson said neither she nor Mr Fortuin will make any further public statements until after this meeting.
