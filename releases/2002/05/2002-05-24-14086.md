Maori Affairs Minister Parekura Horomia welcomes the announcement that Wayne Walden is to assist the Maori Television Service.

"Mr Walden is well known as a man of integrity, who has a long and successful history in business enterprise.

"Mr Walden and his team will provide advice to strengthen the Service.

"This will allow the Maori Television Service to move forward and focus on its business of getting a quality television service to air.

"I am pleased that Mr Walden has agreed to assist the Service and I look forward to his involvement in making Maori Television an 'on air' reality," Parekura Horomia said.
