ministers:
  - Matt Robson

Minister responsible for overseas development, Matt Robson will travel to East Timor with the Prime Minister and Foreign Affairs Minister Phil Goff this weekend.

After the country’s independence celebrations on Sunday evenning, Matt Robson will visit schools and other New Zealand aid projects in the regions.

He will also visit the new prisons which have been established with the help of New Zealand prison staff.

On Monday 20 May, Matt Robson will attend the first meeting of East Timor’s new parliament, and later the ceremony to sign multinational treaties like the Treaty on the non-Proliferation of Nuclear Weapons, and international human rights treaties.

On Tuesday 21 May, Matt Robson will host a reception at the New Zealand Official Residence for high level East Timorese government officials, United Nations officials and New Zealanders working in the region.
