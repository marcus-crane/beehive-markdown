Attorney-General and Labour Minister Margaret Wilson leaves today for Switzerland and the Caribbean.

She will visit the International Labour Organisation (ILO) in Geneva before heading to the Commonwealth Law Ministers Meeting being held in Kingstown, St Vincent and the Grenadines from 18 to 21 November.

Law Ministers meet about every three years and topics for discussion at this meeting will include strategies for enhancing democracy by eliminating legal barriers to development and international co-operation to combat crime.
