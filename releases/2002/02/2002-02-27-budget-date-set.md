27 February 2002Media Statement  
Budget to be delivered on May 23

Finance Minister Michael Cullen will deliver his third budget on Thursday, May 23.

Dr Cullen told the Finance and Expenditure Select Committee the date when he appeared before it today to take questions on the 18 December Budget Policy Statement.
