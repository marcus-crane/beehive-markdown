The Minister of Justice, Hon D.A.M. Graham, today announced the appointment of Hastings Barrister and Solicitor Anthony Patrick Walsh as a District Court Judge.

Judge Walsh will be sworn in at the Hastings District Court on 14 November and will sit in Wanganui with a Family Court warrant from December.

Judge Walsh was admitted to the Bar in 1972 and has since practised as a general practitioner in Hastings. He is currently a partner in a Hastings legal firm, specialising in family law.

He is a former President, Vice President and member of the Hawkes Bay District Law Society and a former member of the Hawkes Bay Legal Aid Committee.

Judge Walsh spent five years as Secretary of the Hawkes Bay Medical Legal Society and six years as a member of the NZ Law Society Family Law Committee.

In 1994 he was a member of the Child Support Working Party. He has contributed to the Trapski Family Law Publication and been a presenter at a NZ Law Society seminar on Child Support.
