The Minister of Justice, Hon D.A.M. Graham, today announced the appointment of Auckland Barrister Laurence John Ryan as a District Court Judge.

Judge Ryan will be sworn in at Auckland District Court on 3 April and will sit at Whangarei from 29 April.

Judge Ryan was admitted to the Bar in 1973 and practised in Auckland as a staff solicitor, sole practitioner, partner and senior litigation partner.

Since 1988 he has practised as a Barrister Sole specialising in family and matrimonial law. He has considerable experience in criminal and civil jurisdictions.

Judge Ryan is senior counsel on the panel of counsel for children in the Auckland region.

He has been a member of the District Legal Services Sub-Committee since 1977.

He was a member of the Auckland District Law Societys Family Court Liaison Committee from its inception until 1995, he is a former convenor of the ADLS Family Law Sub-Committee and he is currently a costs reviser.

Judge Ryan has been a member of various other Auckland District Law Society sub-committees including District Court, Magistrates Court, Legal Aid and Family Law.
