Transport Minister Steven Joyce has today welcomed further progress towards the completion of Auckland's Western Ring Route - which was earlier this year identified as a Road of National Significance.

The NZ Transport Agency has today confirmed plans to widen Auckland's Northwestern Motorway.

This work complements the building of the Waterview Connection and, when it's finished, will complete a major section of the Western Ring Route, and upgrade West Auckland's access to the central city.

Mr Joyce says the completion of the route is essential to the continued growth and economic development in the Auckland region.
