The Minister of Justice, Simon Power, will this evening present New Zealand’s National Report to the United Nations Human Rights Council in Geneva. 

Mr Power will appear as part of New Zealand’s participation in the Universal Periodic Review.

He will make an opening statement addressing the challenges facing New Zealand and detailing some of the initiatives by the Government to assist vulnerable citizens. He will then answer questions from member and observer states of the Human Rights Council.

A copy of New Zealand’s National Report is available at:
