Defence Minister Wayne Mapp will spend a day with South Island Army reservists training in the field near Christchurch on Sunday 15 February.

Dr Mapp will tour Exercise Southern Kiwi, an annual week-long field exercise, on his arrival at the Army's West Melton Rifle Range and will be briefed and have a ride in one of the Army's Light Armoured Vehicles (LAV).
