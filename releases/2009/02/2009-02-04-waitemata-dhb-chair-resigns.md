Health Minister Tony Ryall today thanked Waitemata District Health Board Chair Kay McKelvie for her long service to the DHB. Ms McKelvie has announced her resignation.

"Ms McKelvie's commitment to the DHB and her long service in the Chair position were reflected in her recent appointment as a Companion of the Queen's Service Order," Mr Ryall said.

"I thank Ms McKelvie for her years of public service and wish her well in the future."
