Prime Minister John Key says tonight's historic win by the All Whites has the whole country cheering.

"I wish I was there to celebrate with you, but rest assured I will be raising a toast to you from APEC in Singapore," Mr Key says.

The All Whites beat Bahrain one nil in Wellington tonight, qualifying for the FIFA World Cup finals in South Africa in 2010 - something they have not achieved since 1982.
