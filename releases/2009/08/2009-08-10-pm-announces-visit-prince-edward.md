Prime Minister John Key has announced that His Royal Highness, the Earl of Wessex will visit New Zealand in November.

Prince Edward will visit Christchurch, Wellington and Auckland during his four-day visit.

While in New Zealand Prince Edward will present Gold Awards for the Duke of Edinburgh’s Award Programme.

The Earl of Wessex will be in New Zealand from 2 November until 5 November.

Further details of His Royal Highness’ programme will be available closer to the visit.
