title: "Delamere's Support Assured For Full Term, Says Pm"
date: 1998-10-27

ministers:

"I have spoken to the Hon Tuariki Delamere and he has given me a categorical assurance that his support remains and will continue to remain with the National-led Government," Prime Minister Jenny Shipley said today.

"He has given me a commitment to maintaining stability and support for the full-term of this Government."
