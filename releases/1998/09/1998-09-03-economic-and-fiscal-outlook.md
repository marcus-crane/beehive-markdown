The Treasury has prepared a set of economic and fiscal forecasts for the Cabinet, which will be considered by Cabinet at its meeting on Monday, 7 September.

Following Cabinet consideration, the Treasury will release the forecasts to the public at 10.15 am on Tuesday, 8 September.

A lock-up for media and analysts will commence at 9.30 am on Tuesday, 8 September in the 14th Floor Conference Room, Treasury, No 1 The Terrace, Wellington. It will conclude at 10.15am.

Treasury officials will be present to provide assistance. The Treasurer will attend for part of the lock-up to comment and take questions.

The booklet containing the forecasts will be available at bookshops from 10.15am.
