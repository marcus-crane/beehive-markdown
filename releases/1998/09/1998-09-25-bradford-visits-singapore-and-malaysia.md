Hon Max Bradford, the Minister of Defence, Minister for Enterprise and Commerce, and Minister of Revenue, will visit Singapore and Malaysia next week.

Mr Bradford departs this Sunday (September 27) and returns on Saturday (October 3).

Mr Bradford's visit is both as Minister of Defence and Minister for Enterprise and Commerce.
