Associate Health Minister, Hon Tuariki Delamere, has been elected to chair the main session at the 12th Commonwealth Ministers' conference being held in Barbados.

The conference is attended by Health Ministers and other delegates from 40 Commonwealth countries, including Britain, Canada and Australia.

The sessions being chaired by Mr Delamere will cover topics such as:

- strategic planning and agenda setting in the health sector
- human resource and capacity building
- quality assurance, monitoring and evaluation
- sustainable health information
- financing the health sector.
