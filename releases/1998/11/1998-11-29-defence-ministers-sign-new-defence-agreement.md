Defence Minister Max Bradford and the Australian Minister for Defence, John Moore, will sign a new defence agreement in Melbourne today.

The Status of Forces Agreement will be signed in the historic War Cabinet Room at Victoria Barracks.

The Agreement addresses issues relating to overseas visits by defence forces.

The media is invited to attend at 2.45pm (Australian time) at Victoria Barracks, Melbourne.
