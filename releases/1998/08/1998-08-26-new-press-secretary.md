Prime Minister Jenny Shipley today announced the appointment of Marnie Woodd to the position of Prime Ministerial Press Secretary.

In recent times, Marnie Woodd has served as the Press Secretary for the Minister of Social Welfare, War Pensions and Associate Minister of Health, Roger Sowry. She joins the Prime Minister's Private Office staff with a background in politics and communications, including working in the National Party Parliamentary Research Unit and a communications company.

The Prime Minister says she's very pleased she's been able to attract to her team the services of someone with such excellent writing and communication skills.

Marnie takes up her position in the Prime Minister's Office next Thursday, 3 September.
