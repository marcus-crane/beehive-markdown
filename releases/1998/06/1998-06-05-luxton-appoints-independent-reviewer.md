The Minister of Fisheries, Hon John Luxton, today announced the appointment of Tony Hartevelt as the independent reviewer of the Fisheries Act 1996.

Mr Hartevelt replaces Dr Brent Wheeler who recently resigned from the position.

Trained as an accountant, Mr Hartevelt had 15 years business management experience before joining accounting firm Coopers and Lyrand in 1986.

A partner in the company since 1988, Mr Hartevelt is currently in charge of the firm ?s management consultancy practice and has led several strategy, change and performance assignments for both private and public sector clients.
