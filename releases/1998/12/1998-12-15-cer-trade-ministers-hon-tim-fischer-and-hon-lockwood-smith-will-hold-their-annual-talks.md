CER Trade Ministers Hon Tim Fischer and Hon Lockwood Smith will hold their annual talks in Wellington on Thursday 17 December 1998.

There will be a photo opportunity in the Cabinet Committee Room (8.4 Beehive) at 9.40 am when Mr Fischer and Dr Smith arrive for the talks. The Cabinet Committee Room will be accessible to media from 9.30 am.

At the conclusion of the talks, there will be a media conference in the Beehive Theatrette at 3.15 pm. The theatrette will be accessible to media from 3.00 pm.
