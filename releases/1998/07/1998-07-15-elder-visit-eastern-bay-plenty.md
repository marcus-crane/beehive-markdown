date: 1998-07-15

ministers:
  - Jack Elder
portfolios:
  - Civil Defence

Civil Defence Minister Jack Elder will be visiting the eastern Bay of Plenty tomorrow to inspect the damage caused by the recent heavy rain.

Mr Elder will be arriving at Whakatane Airport at 11.30, and travelling by helicopter to Waimana for a meeting with people involved in the relief effort. The damage near Ruatoki and other areas will also be assessed from the helicopter.
