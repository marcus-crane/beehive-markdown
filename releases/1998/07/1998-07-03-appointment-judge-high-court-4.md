The Attorney-General, Rt Hon D.A.M. Graham, announced today the appointment of Colin Maurice Nicholson, District Court Judge, as a temporary Judge of the High Court to take effect from 1 August 1998.

It is intended that Judge Nicholson will become a permanent Judge of the High Court on the retirement of Justice Smellie.

Judge Nicholson was educated at Auckland University College and graduated from the University of New Zealand with a Bachelor of Laws. He joined the firm of Meredith Meredith Kerr and Cleal, becoming a partner in 1965. He commenced practice as a barrister sole in 1977 and was appointed a Queen's Counsel in 1979. He was appointed a District Court Judge in 1995.

The new Judge will sit in Auckland.
