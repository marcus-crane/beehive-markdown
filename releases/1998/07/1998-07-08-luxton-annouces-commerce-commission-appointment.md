The Minister of Commerce, Hon John Luxton, announced an appointment to the Commerce Commission today. Ms Cathie Harrison has been appointed as a member of the Commission for a period of three years.

Ms Harrison, of Wellington, has been an associate member of the Commission since May 1996.

Ms Harrison is a commercial and litigation lawyer. She is the principal of Harrisons, a specialist media and intellectual property law firm she founded in 1995. Prior to that Ms Harrison was a partner in the law firm Chapman Tripp Sheffield Young.
