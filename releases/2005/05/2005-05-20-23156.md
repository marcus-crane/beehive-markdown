Minister of Transport Pete Hodgson today announced the appointment of Paul Fitzharris to the Board of Land Transport New Zealand.

“Mr Fitzharris has represented Police on the National Road Safety Committee and, before retirement, was an Acting Deputy Commissioner with New Zealand Police. His expertise in land transport safety issues will be an asset to the Board,” says Pete Hodgson.

Notes to Editors:  
Land Transport New Zealand is a Crown entity. It was established on 1 December 2004. Its objective is the promotion of land transport sustainability in New Zealand, to allocate resources and to undertake its functions in a way that contributes to an integrated safe, responsive and sustainable land transport system.
