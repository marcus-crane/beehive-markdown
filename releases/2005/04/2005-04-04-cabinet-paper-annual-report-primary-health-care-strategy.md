A Cabinet Paper (*SDC (04) 174, Primary Health Care Strategy: Monitoring its Achievements*), downloadable in Adobe Acrobat form from this page, provides the first of a series of annual reports requested by Cabinet on the implementation of the [Primary Health Care Strategy](http://www.moh.govt.nz/primaryhealthcare).

On 23 August 2004 Cabinet invited the Minister of Health to report to the Cabinet Social Development Committee annually –- beginning December 2004 and concluding December 2007 –- on:

- the extent to which the Primary Health Care Strategy is meeting the Government’s objectives, and
- the development of effective accountability measures to ensure that Primary Health Organisation funding is used as intended, including incentives such as performance-based payments and sanctions those organisations.
