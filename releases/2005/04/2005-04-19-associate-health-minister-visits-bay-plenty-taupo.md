Associate Health and Rural Affairs Minister Damien O'Connor will meet with local health providers in the Bay of Plenty/Taupo tomorrow and Thursday. He'll also attend a Federated Farmers meeting in Rotorua.

Mr O'Connor said he was looking forward to touching base with rural PHOs and hearing about local issues first hand.

"It's always good to meet with providers on the ground and get a real feel for some of the challenges they face, and what they're achieving."

Mr O'Connor's agenda is below – times are indicative.

Venue: Paeroa Medical Centre, 15 Princess Street, Paeroa

Venue: Te Korowai Hauora O Hauraki, 43 Bellmont Street, Paeroa
