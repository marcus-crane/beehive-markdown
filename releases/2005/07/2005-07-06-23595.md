"We want solutions that make Auckland a better place to live and are working hard to achieve this goal. The Fanshawe Street bus lane extension, which opened this week, will save significant commuter time. This is significant for public transport and roading in Auckland, with a 40 per cent increase in peak services subsidised by the Auckland Regional Transport Authority (ARTA)," Judith Tizard said.
