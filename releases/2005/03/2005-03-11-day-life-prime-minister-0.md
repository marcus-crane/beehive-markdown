**08:30am**  
Leave home for airport to fly to Tauranga.

**09:45am**  
Arrive Tauranga.

**10:00am - Otumoetai Intermediate, Tauranga.**  
Prime Minister officially opens the new performing arts centre, technology block and information communications block. Tours the new facilities and has morning tea with guests.

**11:45am**  
Depart Tauranga for Taupo.

**12:20am**  
Arrive Taupo.

**12:40am - Address Meat Workers Union Conference**  
The Prime Minister addresses the Meat Workers Union as a guest speaker at one of their Executive meetings.

**2:00pm**  
Depart Taupo for New Plymouth.

**3:00pm**  
Arrive New Plymouth.

**3:15pm**  
Media Interview with Taranaki Daily News.

**4:00pm - Officially open Taranaki HSE.**  
Taranaki HSE is New Zealand’s first health, safety and environment protection 'one-stop-shop' and is an initiative between the Centre of Applied Engineering NZ, Accident Compensation Corporation and local industry. The Centre will offer wide-ranging safety training, taking pressure off individual businesses needing to adequately train their staff and ensuring a uniform high standard of training.

**5:45pm - Official opening of WOMAD Festival in New Plymouth.**  
The festival featured almost 400 performers from 15 countries. The Prime Minister officially opened WOMAD.

**7:15pm**  
Depart New Plymouth for Auckland

**9:00pm - Pasifika Music Awards, Auckland.**  
The Prime Minister opened the inaugural Pasifika Music Awards which is part of the Pasifika Festival held annually in Auckland. The evening was dedicated to Phil Fuemana, a musician who died of a heart attack recently. The Prime Minister presented the Lifetime Achievement Award to a member of Phil Fuemana’s family.

**10:00pm**  
Depart for home.
