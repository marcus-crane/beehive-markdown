The Pre-Election Economic and Fiscal Update will be released at 11am on Thursday, August 18.

The document will be available under embargo at a lock-up for media and analysts from 10am in the Old Legislative Council Chambers, Parliament Buildings.

Treasury officials will be present to assist with technical inquiries.

Finance Minister Michael Cullen will attend from 10.30am to take questions.
