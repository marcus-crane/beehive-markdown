Tourism Minister Mark Burton today handed over a $72,000 cheque to the McCahon House Trust to go towards displays in the artist's former residence at French Bay in Waitakere City.

The Tourism Facilities Grant will go towards the installation of interpretative displays in the house where McCahon lived with his family between 1953 and 1960.

Mark Burton paid tribute to the Waitakere City Council for gifting the property to the McCahon House Trust and to the trust for their vision in restoring the house, which is due to be open to the public later this year.

He also paid tribute to the Waitakere community for their "commitment to preserving their environment and cultural heritage and to fostering the arts.
