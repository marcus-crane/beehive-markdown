Building and Construction Minister Shane Jones today confirmed appointments for the Plumbers, Gasfitters and Drainlayers (PGD) board.

"I wish the incoming members well and I acknowledge those who are departing and thank them for their two years of service. I'm sure they will maintain their interest in the industry," Mr Jones said.

Outgoing Board members:

Rodney Miller (Chair), Neil Burrow, Graham Owen, David Coxhead, Penny Nicholas, Paul Warren. Flora Gilkison and Murray Willis resigned during their term of appointment.

Members reappointed:

Anthony Salisbury and Marjorie Noble.

New Members appointed: Stephen Parker, Hazel Armstrong, Robin Hapi, Craig Maxwell-Crawshaw, Mark Whitehead.
