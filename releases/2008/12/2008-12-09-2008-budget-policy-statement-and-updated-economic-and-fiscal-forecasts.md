**Lock-up for media and analysts on Thursday 18 December 2008**

The Treasury will publish the Government's Budget Policy Statement and updated Economic and Fiscal forecasts on Thursday 18 December. 

The Treasury will host a lock-up for journalists and analysts **from 9.30am until 11.00am on Thursday 18 December**, at its premises on the fifth floor of No. 1 The Terrace, Wellington. 

[]()Finance Minister Hon Bill English will address the lock-up at 10.30am.  Treasury officials will be available from 9.30am to answer questions and clarify technical matters.    

Journalists and analysts will be asked to remain at the lock-up until its conclusion at 11.00am, and the embargo on the documents will be lifted at that time.   

**Compliance with the embargo provisions will be monitored.  Any organisation found to break embargo provisions may be refused permission to attend future lock-ups.**

Please RSVP by 5pm on Tuesday 16 December by email to [aimee.morris@treasury.govt.nz](mailto:aimee.morris@treasury.govt.nz), advising the name and organisation of the persons attending and any technical requirements.
