Arts, Culture and Heritage Minister Christopher Finlayson has today released five Briefings to the Incoming Minister.

The briefing documents from the Ministry for Culture and Heritage, Creative New Zealand, the New Zealand Film Commission, the New Zealand Historic Places Trust and the Museum of New Zealand Te Papa Tongarewa are now available on the Beehive website via the attachment links below.
