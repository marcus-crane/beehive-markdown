Jim Anderton said Mexico was a priority country under the New Zealand Government's Latin America Strategy, and our warm and positive relationship is expanding.

"Agricultural cooperation will be a primary focus of Mr Gonz치lez' visit. An arrangement will be signed to allow greater cooperation between the State of Jalisco and the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry.

"Jalisco has the third largest economy of the Mexican states and is an important agricultural producer. Mr Gonz치lez' visit underlines the scope for partnerships with New Zealand on agri-technology and food production.

Jim Anderton said Mr Gonzalez' visit came in the context of an increased tempo of high level visits between New Zealand and Mexico in the past year, suitably marking the 35th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between our two countries.
