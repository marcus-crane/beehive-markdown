Prime Minister Helen Clark said today that the New Zealand Government welcomes the announcement that Chinese authorities will meet with a representative of the Dalai Lama to discuss issues relating to Tibet.

"New Zealand has [consistently urged](http://www.beehive.govt.nz/release/parliamentary+notice+motion+re+tibet) the Chinese Government to enter into dialogue with the Dalai Lama and his representatives," Helen Clark said.

"I was able to put this position directly to Premier Wen Jiabao when we met earlier this month in Beijing.
