I te rangi nei I puta ngā kōrero a Te Miniita mō ngā Take Māori, a Parekura Horomia, arā, kua piki ake te maha o ngā Tauira Hou ki runga atu I te 14,000, I wawatatia e te Kāwanatanga Reipa-ārahi, kia eke I mua o te mutunga o te tau 2008. Ahakoa kotahi tau kei te toi he kōrero pai kē tēnei mō ngā rangatahi Māori.

I raro I ngā tatauranga hauwhā o Hepetema, o te katoa o ngā Tauira Hou e 14,411, 10.534 o ratou kei te ako tonu, e 3877 o rātou kua whakatutuki I a rātou

akoranga. E 1500 ngā Māori I waenga I a rātou.

“Me te pono o tēnei Kāwanatanga Reipa-ārahi, kei runga I te pakaritanga o ngā whānau ngā huarahi pai mō tātou, me taku whakahīhī mō rātou a tātou rangatahi

kua whakatutuki, kei te mahi tonu rānei I roto I ngā mahi ako,” I kī a Parekura Horomia.

“Mā ia whakangungutanga e para I tētahi huarahi whakamau mō ngā rangatahi, kia pūmau tōna tūranga ki roto I ngā whakawhitinga ōhanga o Aotearoa.”

“I tīmatatia e mātou te hōtaka Whakangungutanga Hou I te tau 2000, hei hanga ake I te raukaha o te whakangungu mahi ki Aotearoa. Ka whakatauria te whāinga kia 14,000 ngā tauira, engari kua eke ki runga ake o tēnā I mua wawe o te wā I whakatauria.”
