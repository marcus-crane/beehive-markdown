Brian Roche, an accountant and partner at PricewaterhouseCoopers, has been named as the seventh trustee on the board of Development West Coast.

“I am delighted that Brian has accepted this position,” Finance Minister Michael Cullen said.

“His very wide-range of expertise, encompassing governance, management and operational matters, will be warmly received by other trustees and the wider Coast community,” Dr Cullen said.
