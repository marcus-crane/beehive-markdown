Auckland Central MP Judith Tizard will represent Prime Minister Helen Clark at a Dawn Parade at the Auckland War Memorial Museum on Anzac Day.

The service in the Auckland Domain will begin at 5.15am in the museum's cenotaph area.  
Ms Tizard will return to her electorate for a parade and service at the Grey Lynn RSA (1 Francis St) at 9.30am. She will lay a wreath and give a brief speech.  
As a Government representative she will lay a wreath at the Auckland RSA's service at 11am at the Auckland War Memorial cenotaph.
