Minister of Corrections, Matt Robson will deliver a key note speech at the Oceanic Regional Heads Of Prisons, meeting at Hawkes Bay Prison tomorrow.

The Conference is a chance for heads of prisons from countries across the Pacific to gather together and discuss and resolve issues of common interest.

13 countries will be represented.

The two key themes of the meeting are professional training and development, and the management of indigenous inmates.

DATE: 11th April 2000

TIME: 3.30pm

VENUE: Hawkes Bay Regional Prison, Bridge Pa Road, Hastings.
