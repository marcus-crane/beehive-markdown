The Local Government Act 1974 requires that the level of remuneration for members of a commission, appointed under section 692W of the Act, be approved by the Minister of State Services under the Fees and Travelling Allowances Act 1951. The Minister of State Services approved an annual remuneration for Mr Kirby, as the Commission acting in place of the Rodney District Council, in the range of $130,000 - $150,000. The total of remuneration paid to Councillors and the Mayor in 1998/99 was $417,664.
