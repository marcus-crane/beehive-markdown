The Hon Margaret Wilson held a series of meetings on 22 December 1999 to discuss details of Labour's employment relations policy. Margaret Wilson met with a number of organisations that day, including the Employers' Federation, CTU and the New Zealand Law Society's Employment Law Committee.  
Margaret Wilson also met with Labour Party interest groups, those who attended that meeting were:

Ross Wilson  
Andrew Little  
Peter Conway  
Lorraine Skiffington  
Darien Fenton  
Mike Smith  
James Ritchie  
Graham Kelly MP  
Helen Kelly  
Laura Cronin

The names of those attending that meeting were not released with Thursday's general release because time pressures did not allow us the courtesy ofchecking with them first.
