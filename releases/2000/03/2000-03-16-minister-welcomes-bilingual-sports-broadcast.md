Education, and Sport, Fitness and Leisure Minister Trevor Mallard has welcomed the announcement from Sky Television that they will trial a bilingual service on its digital platform during this weekend's Warriors Rugby League Game.

Viewers will be able to opt for a Maori or English commentary for the Warriors game against St George Illawarra.

"I congratulate Sky on this initiative," Trevor Mallard said.
