The Minister of Commerce Paul Swain today announced the appointment of Paula Rebstock to the Commerce Commission.

Ms Rebstock has been appointed as Member of the Commission for a period of four years. She has been an Associate Member of the Commission since August 1998.

Paul Swain said Paula Rebstock is an economic advisor and is also a Director of the Foundation for Research, Science and Technology.

"Ms Rebstock comes to us with a wealth of experience," he said.

"She is a former senior government executive, with expertise in micro-economics, labour market and regulatory economics, science and technology.

"I welcome her to her new role," he said.

Paula Rebstock has said she is pleased to be continuing her work with the Commerce Commission.
