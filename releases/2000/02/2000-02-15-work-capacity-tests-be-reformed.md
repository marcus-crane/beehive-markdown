date: 2000-02-15

ministers:
  - Michael Cullen
portfolios:
  - Unassigned Portfolio
---

"Work capacity testing will be reformed under new worker accident compensation legislation," says ACC Minister Dr Michael Cullen.

" The work capacity assessment procedure will be used as it was originally intended - a rehabilitation tool rather than a means of removing a claimant's entitlement to cover.

" National used work capacity testing as a clumsy tool to dump injured workers from ACC's books. The new accident compensation legislation will ensure that injured workers must first go through comprehensive, monitored vocational rehabilitation before they are even considered for work capacity testing.

"Work capacity testing is just one area of worker compensation that is crying out for a serious review. The 1998 legislation is full of inequities that range from the downright unfair to the plain daft. How was it that the previous

Government managed to decide, contrary to all laws of physics, that gravity is not a force that can cause accidents?

"I am absolutely confident that the rebuilt accident compensation scheme, with a new focus on injury prevention and rehabilitation, will deliver a better and fairer deal for both workers and employers."
