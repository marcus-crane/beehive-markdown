Deputy Prime Minister Jim Anderton will attend Waitangi Day commemorations at Orongomai marae in Upper Hutt.

The day's features at the marae will include the release of 'Goodwill pigeons' to mark peace and harmony among all cultures followed by cultural entertainment by Maori Pacific Island and ethnic groups throughout the day.

"Waitangi Day is a day for all New Zealanders, and I hope everyone takes the opportunity to participate in and enjoy the activities that make our country special," Jim Anderton said.

Venue: Orongomai marae, 1 Railway Ave, Upper Hutt

9.30AM Powhiri  
10.30AM Release of Goodwill pigeons  
11.00AM Cultural entertainment commences
