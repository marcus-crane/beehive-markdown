The Minister of Transport Mark Gosche is to meet with Mr Liu Jianfeng, Minister for Civil Aviation Administration of China on Monday 14 February.

Mr Jianfeng and a delegation of senior aviation officials from the People's Republic of China are visiting New Zealand this month.
