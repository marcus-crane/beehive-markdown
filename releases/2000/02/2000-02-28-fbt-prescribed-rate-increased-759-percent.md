The rate of interest to calculate the fringe benefit value of low interest employment related loans has be increased to 7.59 percent for the quarter commencing 1 April, 2000.

Finance and Revenue Minister Michael Cullen said the new rate was a reflected recent changes in market rates.

The prescribed rate was last changed in January, 1999.
