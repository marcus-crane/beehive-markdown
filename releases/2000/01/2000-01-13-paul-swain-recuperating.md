Commerce Minister and Rimutaka MP Paul Swain will need several weeks to fully recover from a recent illness, Prime Minister Helen Clark said today.

"During this time Trevor Mallard will be acting minister for Mr Swain..

"In addition to Commerce, Mr Swain holds the Communications, Information Technology, Land Information and Statistics portfolios.

"My colleagues and I wish Paul a speedy recovery and look forward to having him back with the team," Helen Clark said.
