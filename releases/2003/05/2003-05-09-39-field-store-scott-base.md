A new field store to be built at Scott Base will be the largest construction project undertaken since the original commissioning of the base in 1956, Foreign Minister Phil Goff said today.

Antarctica NZ will receive $3.9 million from this year's Budget to develop the two-story 1800 square metre field store. It will provide a dedicated heated facility for organising and preparing all Antarctic field research programmes, along with storage for Scott Base field supplies and perishables.
