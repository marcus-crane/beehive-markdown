Jim Anderton has welcomed the decision of the Christchurch City Council to allow Christchurch City Holdings to invest in the expansion and upgrading of the Air New Zealand engineering centre in Christchurch.

“This is an excellent example of a local authority identifying and supporting a major economic facility which benefits their region. The City Council are to be applauded for their commitment to local jobs and long term strategic development,” said Jim Anderton.
