Broadcasting Minister Jonathan Coleman today announced the appointment of Caren Rangi to the board of NZ On Air.

Ms Rangi, a chartered accountant, has extensive public sector experience having worked in range of organisations including the Office of the Auditor General and Audit New Zealand.

She was also a consultant at Public Sector Performance New Zealand and established her own business, Ei Mua Consulting Ltd, in 2008.

Ms Rangi was appointed for a three-year term until 20 April 2013.
