Building and Construction Minister Maurice Williamson today announced the appointment of Mr Grant Benvenuti and Mr Garrett Glynn to the Engineering Associates Registration Board, for terms of two years.

Mr Williamson also announced the reappointment of Dr Robin Dunlop, Ms Fay Johnstone, Ms Wendy Steadman, Mr Graeme Wells, Mr Tony Blackler, Mr Malcolm Wheeler, and Mr Raymond Grant, to the Board, for terms of two years.

Dr Dunlop has been reappointed as Chair, and Ms Johnstone has been reappointed as Deputy Chair.

Mr Wells was nominated for reappointment by the Design Association of New Zealand Inc; Mr Blackler by the New Zealand Institute of Health Estate and Engineering Management; Mr Wheeler by the New Zealand Electronics Institute; and Mr Grant by the New Zealand Institute of Marine and Power Engineers.

Mr Benvenuti was nominated by the New Zealand Electrical Institute, and replaces Mr Harold Knight, who decided not to seek reappointment.

Mr Glynn was nominated by the New Zealand Institute of Refrigeration, Heating, and Air Conditioning Engineers, and replaces Mr Tom Adson, who did not wish to be reappointed.

The Engineering Associates Registration Board is established under the Engineering Associates Act 1961, and is responsible for approving the registration of engineering associates, encouraging improved competence and qualification of engineering technologists, and exercising disciplinary powers when required.

Mr Williamson thanked outgoing members Mr Knight and Mr Adson for their contribution to the Board.
