Building and Construction Minister Maurice Williamson today announced the appointment of Mr Shane Dolan and reappointment of Ms Penny Mudford to the Electrical Workers Registration Board.

Mr Dolan has been appointed for a term of two years and Ms Mudford for a further term of three years.

Mr Dolan has significant experience in the electricity industry, as well as expertise in establishing quality and safety systems and processes.

"I am pleased to welcome Mr Dolan to the Board", Mr Williamson said.

Mr Williamson also expressed his appreciation of Ms Mudford's continuing commitment to the Board, saying "I have valued Ms Mudford's strong strategic and governance skills, as well as her excellent understanding of the importance of education and training as part of ensuring ongoing competence".

The Electrical Workers Registration Board is established under the Electricity Act 1992, and is responsible for registration and licensing of electrical workers, ensuring the competence of practitioners, and exercising disciplinary powers.

Mr Williamson thanked outgoing member Don Pryde for his contribution to the Board over the last seven years.
