Prime Minister John Key announced today that the Governor-General has reappointed the Honourable Sir John Jeffries as Commissioner of Security Warrants for a further three year term.

Sir John, a former High Court Judge, has served in the role since 1999.

His appointment was made after consultation with the Leader of the Opposition, as is required by statute.
