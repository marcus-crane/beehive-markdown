Barrister Mary Peters has been appointed a Judge of the High Court, Attorney-General Christopher Finlayson announced today.

Ms Peters graduated with an LLB from Victoria University of Wellington in 1985. She practised with Russell McVeagh in Auckland between 1995 and 2004, being made partner in 1997, having previously practised in London and New York, for Arthur Andersen & Co and Allen & Overy, respectively.  

Most recently as a barrister Ms Peters has predominantly undertaken civil work with particular interest in land law, trust and estate matters.

She will be sworn in at Auckland on 20 August 2010.
