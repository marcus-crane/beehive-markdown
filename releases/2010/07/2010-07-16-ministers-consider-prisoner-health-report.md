The Ministers of Health and Corrections, Tony Ryall and Judith Collins have thanked the National Health Committee for their report *Health in justice: Improving the health of prisoners, their families and whanau.* 

Ministers Ryall and Collins said they will be asking the Ministry of Health and Department of Corrections for formal advice on the report.

A copy of the report is available at  [http://www.nhc.health.govt.nz/moh.nsf/indexcm/nhc-health-in-justice](http://www.nhc.health.govt.nz/moh.nsf/indexcm/nhc-health-in-justice)
