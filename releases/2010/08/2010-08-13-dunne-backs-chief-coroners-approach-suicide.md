Associate Health Minister Peter Dunne said today that he is "sympathetically disposed" to the calls from the Chief Coroner, Judge Neil MacLean, for greater openness around the reporting and discussion of suicide.

"I am very open to what he is saying," Mr Dunne said.

"I think there needs to be more discussion about exactly how what he is proposing might work.
