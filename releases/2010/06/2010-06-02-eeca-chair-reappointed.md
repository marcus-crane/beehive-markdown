Energy and Resources Minister Gerry Brownlee has announced the reappointment of Roger Sutton as chair of the Energy Efficiency and Conservation Authority (EECA) board.

Mr Sutton has been a member of the EECA board since 2005 and chair since 2007.

"Roger Sutton has served EECA well, bringing extensive experience in the energy industry and knowledge of energy efficiency and renewable energy," Mr Brownlee said.

"I wish him well in his new term."

Mr Brownlee is also inviting applications for new members to sit on the EECA board.  Further information can be found at www.med.govt.nz/crown-entity-vacancies

**Background:**

Roger Sutton is chief executive of Orion New Zealand and is a director of 4RF Communications.  He has previously been a director of Australian energy companies Energetics and Energy Developments.  He has also been a trustee of Community Energy Action, a Christchurch based trust that supports energy efficiency initiatives for low income households.
