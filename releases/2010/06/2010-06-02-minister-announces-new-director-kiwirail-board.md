State Owned Enterprises Minister Simon Power today announced the appointment of John Leuchars as a director of KiwiRail. 

Mr Leuchars' appointment is effective from 1 July 2010 to 30 April 2013.

"Mr Leuchars brings strong technical engineering skills aligned with major project management and governance experience to the board," Mr Power said.

"I look forward to the contribution he will make to KiwiRail as it further develops its strategy over the coming years."

Mr Leuchars replaces Linda Constable whose term ends on 30 June 2010.

He will start on the board at the same time as the new chair, John Spencer. 

Mr Power thanked Ms Constable for her work over the past seven years, formerly on the board of Ontrack, and more recently KiwiRail.

Background

John Leuchars is a professional company director who is a civil engineer by training, and has significant experience in senior management of international consulting engineering companies.  He has governance experience in a number of large engineering consultancy firms and not-for-profit organisations. 

From 1 July 2010, the composition of the KiwiRail board will be: John Spencer (chair), Paula Rebstock (deputy chair), Mark Tume, Bob Field, Bryan Jackson, Mark Franklin and John Leuchars.
