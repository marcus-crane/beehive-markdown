Foreign Minister Winston Peters has announced three new diplomatic appointments:

- **Joanna Kempkers,** Ambassador to France;
- **Greg Andrews,** High Commissioner to Fiji; and
- **Angela Hassan-Sharp,** Ambassador to Ireland.
