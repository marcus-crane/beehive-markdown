Foreign Minister Winston Peters has announced three new diplomatic appointments: 

- **Charlotte Frater,** Ambassador to the Netherlands.
- **Peter Kemp,** High Commissioner to Vanuatu.
- **Stuart Horne,** Consul-General in Honolulu.
