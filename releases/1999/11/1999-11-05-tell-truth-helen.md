"Labour's ad said Catherine Holloway was trying to support seven children on $40 to $50 a week - and she wasn't," said Roger Sowry.

WINZ have made it quite clear she was and is getting the correct benefit, but Labour's ad misrepresented her circumstances.

"Helen Clark shouldn't have to lie to make sense of her policies."
