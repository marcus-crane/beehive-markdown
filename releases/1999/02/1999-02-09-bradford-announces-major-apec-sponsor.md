Enterprise and Commerce Minister Max Bradford announced today that computer software company Jade Group will be a major sponsor of the APEC SME (small to medium sized enterprise) Business Forum and Ministerial Meeting being held in Christchurch in April.

Mr Bradford said as a major sponsor the Jade Group will help promote the image of New Zealand as an information economy.

"APEC New Zealand 99 is very much a partnership with the business community - a partnership for business and employment growth, a partnership for recovery in the region," he said.

"The image of the Jade Group as a new generation technologycompany is the sort of positive business image that we want to promote to the leaders of APEC.

"This image, complemented with essential imput from the business sector as a whole, will help the APEC SME Business Forum andMinisterial Meeting to focus on the expansion of opportunities for doing business throughout the APEC region," Mr Bradford said.
