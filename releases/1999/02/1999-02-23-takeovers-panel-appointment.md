Enterprise and Commerce Minister Max Bradford today announced the appointment of Ms Daphne Rawstorne as a member of the Takeovers Panel for a period of two years.

Ms Rawstorne is a Business Consultant. She was managing partner of the Auckland/Wellington practice of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu from 1991 until 1997.

She is currently Chair of MATPRO, a consortium of Maternity professionals. She is also a member of the Management Board of the Children, Young Persons, and their Families Agency (CYPFA), a Board Member of ECNZ, and a Trustee of the Victoria University Foundation. She was previously a Director of New Zealand Post for nine years. She is a Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of New Zealand and a Member of the Institute of Directors New Zealand.
