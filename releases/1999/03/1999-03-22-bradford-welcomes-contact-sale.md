Enterprise and Commerce (including energy) Minister Max Bradford today welcomed the cornerstone sale of Contact Energy.

In response to media enquiries, Mr Bradford said: "The Government's electricity reforms, which fully come into play from April 1, have already increased competition and given thousands of consumers choice about who they buy their power from and lower prices.

"The cornerstone sale of Contact announced today will bring anew player into the electricity generation and distribution market place, giving the choice to consumers and competition amongst producers needed to make the electricity market the best in the world."
