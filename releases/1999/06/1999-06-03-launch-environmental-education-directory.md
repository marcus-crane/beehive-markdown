ARCHIBALD CENTRE, WELLINGTON ZOO MANCHESTER STREET, NEWTOWN

At 10.00am tomorrow, the Associate Minister for the Environment, the Hon Marie Hasler, will launch the new Environmental Education Directory of New Zealand.

This directory brings together information about what various individuals and organisations are doing in the environmental education field.

At the launch the Minister will also announce thus year's winners of the Green Ribbon Award, an award recognising the work of groups and individuals towards a healthy and sustainable environment.

All media are welcome to attend the launch.
