portfolios:

Minister for Enterprise and Commerce Max Bradford will escort a group of APEC Ministerial colleagues to Queenstown on 28-29th April.

The Ministers will go to Queenstown after the APEC SME (Small and Medium Enterprises) Ministerial meeting being held in Christchurch, 26-28th April.

The meeting will see high ranking Ministers from the Asia Pacific region discuss the issues surrounding the economies of APEC.

After the meeting ends mid Wednesday 28th (April) the Ministers will fly to Queenstown in the "Pionair Classic" DC3, via the Mesopotamia High Country Station.

The Ministers will stay at the Millbrook Resort in Queenstown participating in activities at the complex on Thursday morning, followed by a trip on the Shotover Jet that afternoon.

A photo opportunity for the media has been arranged for Thursday 29th April, 8.30am at Millbrook Resort.
