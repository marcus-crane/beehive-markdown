Enterprise and Commerce Minister Max Bradford will outline legislation to impose controls on electricity lines companies  
during a press conference in his Bowen House 18th floor office at 12.45pm tomorrow (Tuesday May 25).

The legislation, which will stop lines companies over charging, will be introduced into Parliament tomorrow (Tuesday May  
25th).

Information on the legislation will be embargoed until 2pm.

Press kits should be available for the media, under this embargo, an hour before the press conference begins.
