title: "Hercules Leaves For Darwin"
date: 1999-09-09

ministers:
  - Max Bradford
portfolios:
  - Defence
---

The New Zealand Government has agreed to an Australian request for assistance in the possible evacuation of United Nations personnel in Dilli, East Timor, Defence Minister Max Bradford said today.

A New Zealand Defence Force C130 Hercules, which had been on 24-hour standby, today departed for Darwin.

Mr Bradford said the decision to send the Hercules was made this morning.
