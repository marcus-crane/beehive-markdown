The Pre-election Economic and Fiscal Update will be released on Thursday 21 October 1999 at 10am.

A briefing for media and analysts will be held in the 14th Floor Conference Room in the Treasury Building, No 1 The Terrace, Wellington, commencing at  
9am and concluding at 10am when the document will be available to the public.

The Treasurer will attend the lock-up at around 9.40am. Treasury officials will be present from 9.15am to provide assistance.

The documents will be tabled in the House on the next sitting day of Parliament.
