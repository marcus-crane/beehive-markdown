Minister of Food, Fibre, Buiosecurity and Border Control, Hon John Luxton, today formally met with the Australian Agricultural Minister, Hon Mark Vaile, as part of his New Zealand visit.

Mr Luxton said today," New Zealand and Australia are very important to each other. There are a lot of similar issues in the agriculture sector on both sides of the Tasman and it is good to learn from each others experiences."
