The Minister of Justice, Hon D.A.M. Graham, today announced the appointment of Dunedin Barrister and Solicitor Colin John Doherty as a District Court Judge.

Judge Doherty will be sworn in at the Dunedin District Court on 8 April and will sit in Christchurch from 29 April.

Judge Doherty graduated from Otago University BA (Hons) in 1976 and LLB in 1978. He was admitted to the Bar in 1977.

He is a litigation partner with a Dunedin law firm, specialising in civil litigation and resource management.

Judge Doherty has been a member of various Otago Law Society sub committees. He is a faculty member of the NZ Law Society's litigation skills programme.

He is a member of the Resource Management Law Association and LEADR (Lawyers Engaged in Alternative Dispute Resolution). He is a former arbitrator under the Noxious Plants Act and a former tutor of professional units at Otago University.
