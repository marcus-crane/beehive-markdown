The Minister of Transport, Jenny Shipley, today announced the following appointments and reappointments.

Christchurch International Airport Ltd: Mr Ivan Jamieson has been reappointed for a further term as a director, and a new director, Susan Sheldon has been appointed. Mrs Sheldon is a chartered accountant in practice. The Crown has a 25 % shareholding in CIAL.

Invercargill Airport Ltd: The Chairman of the board, Norman McRae, and director Alan Millar have been re-appointed for a further term. Invercargill Airport Ltd is 45 % owned by the Crown.

Mrs Shipley is also pleased to announce two re-appointments to the Board of Radio New Zealand Ltd, in her capacity as Minister Responsible for Radio New Zealand. Denis Dutton and Maria Collins have been re-appointed for a further term.

These appointments have been agreed to by both Coalition partners, National and New Zealand First.
