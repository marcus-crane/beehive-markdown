The Minister of Justice, Hon D.A.M. Graham, today announced the appointment of Auckland Barrister Christopher John Field as a District Court Judge.

Judge Field will be sworn in at the Otahuhu District Court on 7 February and will sit in Auckland from 3 March.

Judge Field graduated LL.B from Auckland University and was admitted to the Bar in 1972. He practised in Manurewa, Papatoetoe and Otahuhu specialising in criminal trial work, civil and family court work. In 1995, he began practice as a Barrister Sole.

Judge Field has been a member of the Prosecutors Panel since 1990 and is a member of the Criminal Bar Association.

He has also served on the Auckland District Law Society's Legal Aid Assignment Committee and its Legal Services Sub-Committee.
