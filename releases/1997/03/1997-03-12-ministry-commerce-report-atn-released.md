Communications Minister, Maurice Williamson has released the Ministry of Commerces report into Aotearoa Television  
network today.

Mr Williamson says the report contains the Ministrys assessment of operations at ATN.

Copies of the report are available from the Ministry of Commerce and on the World Wide Web (minus appendices) at:  
[http://www.govt.nz/comms/tbp/bcast.html](http://www.govt.nz/comms/tbp/bcast.html%20)
