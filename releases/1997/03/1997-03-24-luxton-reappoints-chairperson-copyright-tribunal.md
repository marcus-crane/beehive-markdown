title: "LUXTON REAPPOINTS CHAIRPERSON OF COPYRIGHT TRIBUNAL"
date: 1997-03-24

ministers:
  - John Luxton
portfolios:

The Minister of Commerce, the Hon John Luxton, announced an appointment to the Copyright Tribunal today. Judge AE Gaskell has been reappointed as Chairperson for a period of five years. Her appointment will take effect from 28 September 1996.
