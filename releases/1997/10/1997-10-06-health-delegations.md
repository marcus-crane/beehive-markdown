The following delegations have been agreed in the health portfolio between the Minister of Health, Bill English, and the Associate Minister of Health, Tuariki John Delamere.

Mr English  
Health strategic planning and policy, health and disability services policy, public health policy, health funding management, health information services, CHE monitoring, CHE ownership issues, residual implementation issues, CHE ministerials and parliamentary questions, PHARMAC, strengthening families, child health strategy, health research, workforce issues including clinical training.

Mr Delamere  
Maori health, Maori public health policy, health regulation and protection, occupational registration, food and nutrition, communicable diseases, environmental health (incl. Smokefree Environments Act), transport and accommodation policy, national advisory and support services, Health and Disability Consumer Rights and Advocacy Services (includes the Health and Disability Commissioner and Ethic Committees), health care services for ineligible people whose debts are unrecoverable, international health.

Joint issues  
Mr English and Mr Delamere will work jointly on general ministerial correspondence and Parliamentary questions (in accordance with delegations) appointments and governance, health sector structural change (as signalled in Coalition Agreement), global funding of health sector, blood issues including Hep C/HIV issues arising from the Coalition Agreement.
