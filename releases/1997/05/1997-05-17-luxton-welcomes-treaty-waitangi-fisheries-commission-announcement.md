  - Fisheries and Aquaculture
---

Minister of Fisheries, Hon John Luxton, today welcomed the announcement from the Treaty of Waitangi Fisheries Commission (the Commission) that they have agreed on a framework for final consultation for the allocation of the Commissions pre Settlement assets.

\`\`I am delighted that progress has been made,'' Mr Luxton said today.

\`\`I hope that the final outcome will be fair for all involved. I look forward to the consultation process progressing and to receiving the Commissions final report in due course for consideration,'' Mr Luxton concluded.
