Minister of Commerce, the Hon John Luxton, will announce the outcome of the motor vehicle tariff review, which was brought forward at the request of the car companies, at

Day: Thursday 18 December 1997

Time: 11am

Where: 19th Floor Bowen House, Parliament

(Please note that this announcement only applies to car tariffs, not the General Tariff Review, (clothing, textiles, footwear, and others)which will not be completed until the middle of 1998)
