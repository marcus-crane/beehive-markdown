The Minister of Commerce, the Hon John Luxton, announced an appointment to the Securities Commission today. Mr Stephen Franks has been appointed to the Commission for a period of five years.

Mr Franks, of Wellington, is a partner at Chapman Tripp Sheffield Young and specialises in securities law. In 1991 he was one of four private sector representatives on a Ministerial Group to advice on the Government's securities law reform programme. He is a director of Wrightson Ltd and the Deputy Chairman of the New Zealand Stock Exchange's Market Surveillance Panel.
