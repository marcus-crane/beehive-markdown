---
title: "NZers Advised Against Travelling to Iraq"
date: 1997-11-14
url: https://www.beehive.govt.nz/release/nzers-advised-against-travelling-iraq
ministers:
  - Don McKinnon
portfolios:
  - Foreign Affairs and Trade
---

The Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade, Don McKinnon, tonight advised New Zealanders against travelling to Iraq in light of the current security climate.

Mr McKinnon said any New Zealanders currently living in Iraq, will be aware of the security situation and have contingency plans in place relating to their safety.
