The Minister of Commerce, the Hon John Luxton, announced today the reappointment of Dr Kate Brown to the Commerce Commission for a period of three years.

Dr Brown has been a member of the Commission since July 1995. She is a senior lecturer at the Department of Finance and Quantitative Analysis, University of Otago. Prior to that she was Assistant Professor at the Eastern Connecticut University. Dr Brown also has work experience in the United States as Chief Accountant of a small manufacturer, Linatex Corporation of America.
