The Minister of Justice, Hon D.A.M. Graham, today announced the appointment of District Court Judge Ian Albert Borrin as Deputy Police Complaints Authority.

Judge Borrin's four year-appointment takes effect from 1 October 1997.

Judge Borrin was appointed to the District Court Bench in 1983. He was the Executive Judge for Wellington from 1991 to 1993.

Judge Borrin was a partner in a Wellington law firm before his appointment to the Bench.
