The Prime Minister has announced that, as a mark of respect for Diana Princess of Wales, all New Zealand flags are requested to be flown at half-mast up to and including the day of the funeral. This date is to be advised following an official announcement from the Palace.

An official Condolence Book will be opened at 1pm today in the main foyer of Parliament Buildings, Wellington, and also at the main gates of Government House, Auckland, 119 Mountain Road, Epsom.

Flowers can be left either at the main steps of Parliament or outside the British High Commission, 44 Hill Street, Wellington.
