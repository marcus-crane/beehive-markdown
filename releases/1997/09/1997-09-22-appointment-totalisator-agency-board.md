The Minister for Racing, the Hon Tau Henare, today announced the appointment of Alistair Sutherland to serve on the Totalisator Agency Board.

\`Mr Sutherland is a member of the Racing Industry Board and, in accordance with the Racing Act, was nominated by that Board to be its representative on the TAB,' says Mr Henare.

Mr Sutherland has a range of experience in a number of areas including industrial relations advocacy, manufacturing, operational management and community fund raising. He was previously Chairperson and Managing Director of Phillips NZ Ltd. He is currently the Chief Executive Officer of Morgan & Banks Ltd, a position that he has held since 1 March 1997. Mr Sutherland was appointed to the Racing Industry Board in July 1996.

\`\`Mr Sutherland has the necessary knowledge, skills and experience to perform the functions and duties of a member of the TAB'', said Mr Henare. \`\`I am confident that he will bring additional business and marketing skills to the Board''.
