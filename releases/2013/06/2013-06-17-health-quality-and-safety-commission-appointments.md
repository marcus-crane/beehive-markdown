The Government has made three appointments to the Health Quality and Safety Commission.

Shelley Frost, an HQSC board member since 2010, has been appointed as Deputy Chair. Mrs Frost is Director of Nursing, Pegasus Health, Chair of General Practice New Zealand and a Trustee of Partnership Health Canterbury PHO.

Dr Dale Bramley (Ngā Puhi) has been appointed as an HQSC board member for a three year term. Mr Bramley is a public health physician and Chief Executive of Waitemata District Health Board.

Alison Paterson also becomes a board member. Mrs Paterson is a chartered accountant and Chair of BPAC New Zealand Limited, NZ Formulary Limited and the governing Board of the Centre of Research Excellence for Growth and Development (University of Auckland). She is a director of Vector Limited, and a former board member of the Reserve Bank of New Zealand. Mrs Paterson is also a former Chair of Waitemata Health Ltd (1996-2001), and of Landcorp.
