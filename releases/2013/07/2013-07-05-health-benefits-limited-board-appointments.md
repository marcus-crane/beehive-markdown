Three directors have been reappointed to the Board of Health Benefits Ltd (HBL).

They are Chair Ted van Arkel, Deputy Chair Dr Lester Levy and director Chris Fleming (CEO Nelson Marlborough DHB).

Murray Georgel has been appointed as director replacing Tracey Adamson who retired in April. Murray Georgel is the CEO of Mid-Central DHB.

Paula Rebstock is retiring from the Board and the Government thanks her for her contribution.

Health Benefits Ltd was established in 2010 to help the 20 district health boards save money by reducing their administrative, support and procurement costs.
