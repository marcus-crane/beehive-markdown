Economic Development Minister Steven Joyce and Trade Minister Tim Groser today announced the appointment of Jennifer Kerr to the New Zealand Trade and Enterprise Board.

Ms Kerr has been appointed for a three-year term from June 2013.

Ms Kerr was formerly Group Director Human Resources at Fonterra and has worked in human resources in the oil industry in the United States and Europe and the leisure industry in the United Kingdom.

“Jennifer Kerr is well placed to take up the role as a member of the NZTE Board. She has extensive international business experience, and brings a strong background in strategy development and HR leadership,” the Ministers say.

The Ministers thanked outgoing member, Peter Conway, for his service to the Board.

“As a longstanding board member Peter Conway has made a valuable contribution to the strategic direction and operation of NZTE.”

Biography for Jennifer Kerr

Jennifer Kerr is a Hamilton-based consultant and director for j-RH Coaching and Consultancy. Jennifer was formerly Group Director Human Resources at Fonterra where her role included responsibility for Fonterra's 17,000 staff worldwide. She has worked in human resources in the oil industry in the US and Europe and the leisure industry in the United Kingdom.

She has a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Waikato and is currently completing a graduate diploma in Psychology.
