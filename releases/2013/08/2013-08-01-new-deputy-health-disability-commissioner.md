Health Minister Tony Ryall has announced the appointment of Ms Rose Wall, as a Deputy Health and Disability Commissioner.

“A nurse by training, Ms Wall has extensive experience and knowledge of public health services including working at Richmond Services, the Ministry of Health and Capital & Coast District Health Board,” says Mr Ryall.

“Her experience has given her a broad insight into the health and disability sectors, including the regulatory, funding and operational areas.”

Ms Wall replaces Ms Tania Thomas and will begin her five year term on 26 August 2013.
