Minister of Trade Tim Groser has announced that, on behalf of the twelve members of the Trans-Pacific Partnership (TPP), New Zealand has released the text of the results of TPP negotiations in its capacity as Depositary of the Agreement.

The text is currently available on the international treaty depository section of the New Zealand Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade’s website, at [www.mfat.govt.nz/tppdepositary](http://www.mfat.govt.nz/tppdepositary).

The text will continue to undergo legal review and will be translated into French and Spanish language versions prior to signature.
