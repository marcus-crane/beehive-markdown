Economic Development Minister Steven Joyce and Trade Minister Tim Groser today announced new appointments and reappointments to the New Zealand Trade and Enterprise (NZTE) Board.

Wayne Norrie and Charlotte Walshe are new appointments to the Board. Andrew Ferrier (Chair), Charles Finny, Karen Fistonich and Robin Hapi have been reappointed to the Board, and Julie Christie and Karl Wixon have been reappointed to the New Zealand Story Advisory Board.

Mr Norrie is Chairman of the Hi Tech Trust, Maven, and Online Republic. He has previously chaired the NZTE Beachhead programme. Mr Norrie has more than 30 years’ experience in technology, finance and consulting services, and international experience in Australia, the UK and the US. He is a Chartered Fellow of the Institute of Directors, a member of the World Class New Zealander network, and a Fellow of the NZ Institute of Management.

Ms Walshe is CEO and Director at Dynamic Controls, a Christchurch-based designer, manufacturer and marketer of medical mobility control devices. She is also a Director at Invacare Holdings and at Enable Services Ltd. She has previously served as Chair and Deputy Chair for the Ministry of Science and Innovation’s Sector Advisory and Funding Advisory Groups, and has previously been a consultant in business strategy and mentoring.
