Prime Minister Helen Clark today announced the appointment of Acting Ministers in portfolios, in the wake of the resignation of Hon David Benson-Pope.

Hon Steve Maharey is Acting Minister of Social Development and Employment.

Hon David Parker is Acting Minister for the Environment.
