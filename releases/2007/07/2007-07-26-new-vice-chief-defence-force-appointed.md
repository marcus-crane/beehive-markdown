Defence Minister Phil Goff announced today that Rear Admiral Jack Steer will become the next Vice Chief of Defence Force (VCDF).

Rear Admiral Steer, a navy officer, is currently the Commander of Joint Forces and he will take up his new appointment in February 2008, following the retirement of Air Vice-Marshal David Bamfield, the current VCDF.

Rear Admiral Steer was born in Christchurch and joined the navy in 1973. He has held a variety of appointments including commanding the frigate HMNZS Wellington during an operational deployment in the Arabian Gulf and Military Adviser to the New Zealand Mission to the UN.
