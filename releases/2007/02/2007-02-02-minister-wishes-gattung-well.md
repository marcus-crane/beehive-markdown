Communications Minister David Cunliffe wishes Telecom chief executive Theresa Gattung well following the announcement of her resignation.

"Theresa Gattung has been one of New Zealand's leading business figures and a leading businesswoman," Mr Cunliffe says.

"I have had frequent contact with her during my time as Associate Minister and Minister of Communications. I've enjoyed this interaction and I respect her energy and passion highly.

"I wish her well for all her future endeavours."
