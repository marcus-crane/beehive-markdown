Acting Pacific Island Affairs Minister Luamanuvao Winnie Laban is deeply saddened by the tragedy that has struck the family of Samoan Mangere resident Folole Muliaga.

“I would like to pass on my condolences to the family of Mrs Muliaga and all of the Mangere community during this sad time. Contact has been made with the family to ensure they have the support they need,” said Laban.

Police are investigating the death. The State Owned Enterprise (SOE) Mighty River Power, the parent company of Mercury Energy, has also launched an investigation.

“The Pacific community can be assured that the government is taking this issue very, very seriously.

“The full picture about what happened is still unclear and as the matter is now in the hands of Police it is not appropriate to jump to conclusions at this time,” said Laban.

Samoan translation:

Fa’amaisega a le Afioga i le Minista mo le aiga fa’anoanoa i totonu o Magele

Ua fa’aalia e le afioga i le Minista ia Hon Luamanuvao Winnie Laban lona fa’anoanoa tele i le tulaga ua tula’i mai i le tu’umalo o le tina ia Folole Muliaga o Magele.

Saunoa le afioga I le Minista e momoli lana fa’amaisega i le tama o le aiga le susuga ia Lopa Muliaga aemaise le fanau ma le aiga fa’anoanoa, fa’apea tatou tagata i totonu o le itumalo o Magele. Ua iai le feso’otaiga a lo’u ofisa ma le aiga pe afai e mo’omia se fesoasoani.

O lo’o galulue nei le ofisa o Leoleo i le su’esu’eina o le tulaga o le maliu o le Tina. O le State Owned Enterprise Mighty River Power po’o le kamupani o lo’o pulea le Mercury Energy o lo’o su’esu’eina foi le tulaga lava lea e tasi.

Toe saunoa le afioga I le Minista ia Luamanuvao Winnie Laban “o lo’o fa’atâuaina tele e le malo le tulaga ua tula’i mai i le maliu o lenei Tina. Tatou tâlia su’esuega a Leoleo ina ia vave fa’ama’eina”
