A $30,000 a year scholarship is being created to encourage te reo Mâori speakers working in other careers to retrain as teachers, Associate Education Minister Parekura Horomia announced today.

Mr Horomia said the current range of teacher recruitment scholarships are being replaced with a single, flexible and more responsive scholarship.

“Of particular significance is the enhanced scholarship for prospective teachers in Mâori medium and te reo Mâori. As well as paying course costs, the enhanced scholarship offers a $30,000 annual allowance to people from other careers to retrain in teaching areas of high demand.”

To be eligible for the scholarship, applicants need fluency in te reo Mâori and either three years work experience and a degree (for the te reo Mâori scholarship) or six years work experience (for the Mâori medium scholarship).

"Existing scholarships offer a maximum allowance of $10,000, with some also covering course fees. The change is worth up to nine times the current allowance over the period of study.

"It aims to increase incentives for te reo speakers in other professions to make the switch into teaching. In order to meet the goals of the Labour-led government's Mâori Language Strategy more te reo teachers are necessary," Mr Horomia said.

"I have initiated the establishment of a think tank designed to further develop a vision for Mâori medium education, including a long term plan for the recruitment and retention of Mâori medium teachers.

"The revised scholarship should assist increased teacher supply and will be an enormous boost to the teaching of Mâori in both primary and secondary schools. Demand is there for people with wider experience to come into teaching."

Mr Horomia said applications for the new scholarships will be available from next month, with the first recipients to study in 2008. Recipients will be bonded to take up teaching following their training.

I pânuitia e Parekura Horomia, Te Minita Tuarua mo te Mâtauranga i te râ nei, te waihangatanga o tçtahi karahipi mô te $30,000 i te tau, mô te akiaki i ngâ tângata e matatau ana ki te kôrero i te reo Mâori kei roto i çra atu umanga e mahi ana, ki te whakangungu anô i a râtou hei kaiako.

He ai ki a Parekura Horomia, kei te whakakorea atu ngâ karahipi kimi kaimahi o tçnei wâ, e tçtahi karahipi kotahi e ngâwari ana, â, e mâhorahora ake ana.

“Ko tçtahi tino tikanga, ko te whakareinga ake o te karahipi mô ngâ kaiako o te reo Mâori i runga i ngâ reo irirangi me ngâ pouaka whakaata. Tâpiri atu ki te utu i ngâ whakararu e pâ ana ki ngâ whakangungutanga, kei te tuku tçnei karahipi i te $30,000 i ia tau ki ngâ tângata mai i çrâ atu umanga ki te whakangungu anô i a râtou hei kaiako i roto i ngâ wâhanga whakaako e tino hiahiatia ana e râtou.

Mô tô âhei ki te tono mo tçnei karahipi, me matatau ngâ kaitono ki te kôrero i te reo Mâori, kia toru tau rânei e mahi ana i roto i tçtahi mahi, he tohu mâtauranga rânei mô te Karahipi o Te Reo Mâori, â, e ono tau rânei e mahi ana mô te karahipi papâho i roto i te reo Mâori.

“Ko ngâ karahipi o tçnei wâ kei te tuku tahua mo te $10,000 taara, â, ko çtahi hoki kei te utu mô tô whakamâtautau i tçnei kaupapa. Ko tçnei whakarerekçtanga kei runga ake i te iwa tâima o te tahua kei te utuhia i tçnei wâ mo te wâ e whakamâtautau ana koe i tçnei kaupapa.

“Ko te tûmanako, ko te whakangâkau nui i ngâ kaikôrero o te reo Mâori kei roto i çrâ atu umanga e mahi ana, ki te tahuri mai ki roto i te ao o te kaiwhakaako. He ai ki a Parekura Horomia, kia tutuki ai ngâ whâinga a Te Rautaki mô Te Reo Mâori e ârahitia nei e Te Kâwana o Reipa, e tika ana kia whiwhi tâtou i çra atu kaiako mô te reo Mâori.

“Kua tîmataria e au te whakaûnga o tçtahi rôpu whâiti hei whanake atu i te matakite mô te mâtauranga mai i te reo Mâori, â, tâpiri atu ki tçtahi mahere mô te whâinga roa mô te kimi me te pupuri i ngâ kaiako o te reo Mâori.

Kaore e kore, ka âwhinatia e tçnei karahipi kua whakapaitia ake nei, te whakamahatanga atu o ngâ kaiako mô te whakaako i te reo Mâori i roto i ngâ kura tuatahi me ngâ kura tuarua. Kei reira ngâ hiahiatanga mo ngâ tangata e whânui ana ngâ wheako ki te haere mai ki roto i te ao o te kaiwhakaako.

He ai ki a Parekura Horomia, ka âhei te tangata ki te tono mô çnei karahipi hou mai i tçnei marama e hekemai nei, â, ko ngâ kaitono tuatahi e whiwhwi ana i te karahipi ka tîmata i a râtou rangahau a te tau 2008.
