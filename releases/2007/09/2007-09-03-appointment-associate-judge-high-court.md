District Court Judge David Robinson has been appointed a temporary Associate Judge of the High Court, Attorney-General Dr Michael Cullen announced today.

Associate Judge Robinson graduated from Auckland University. He was appointed a District Court Judge in 1983, serving as a Family Court Judge from 1983 until 2006.

Associate Judge Robinson has been appointed for a term of one year and will sit in Auckland.
