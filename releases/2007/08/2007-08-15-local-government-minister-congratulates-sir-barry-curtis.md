Local Government Minister Mark Burton today congratulated Sir Barry Curtis, for his outstanding contribution to his Manukau community, following Sir Barry's announcement that he would be standing down at the next local body elections.

"During his time in local body politics Sir Barry has set a benchmark for civic leadership, and displayed unwavering passion and dedication to his community - as evident today as it was when he was first elected " Mark Burton said

"It is to be hoped that Sir Barry's example will be a stimulus for candidates in the upcoming local body elections, certainly any local body politician would do well to emulate his many years of dedicated service," he said

"I am sure Sir Barry Curtis will continue to make a valuable contribution to his Manukau community and to the wider community of New Zealand.
