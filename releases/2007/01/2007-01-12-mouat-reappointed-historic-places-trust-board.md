Judith Tizard, Associate Arts, Culture and Heritage Minister, has reappointed Murray Mouat to the New Zealand Historic Places Trust Board.

In announcing his appointment the Minister acknowledged Murrary Mouat’s contribution and commitment to the Trust and welcomed his willingness to continue to serve on the Trust Board.

“His knowledge of property management and properties of historic importance and his business and governance experience have added considerable value during his ten years on the Trust Board.”

Mr Mouat’s term ends on 30 September 2007.

Background Information

Murray Mouat has an extensive knowledge of properties of historic importance on a regional and national basis. He owns and manages his own property management consultancy in Invercargill (since 1987) and is also Southland Regional Account Manager for DHL Worldwide Express (since 1989). He has wide governance experience in the private and public sector and has been a member of the New Zealand Historic Places Trust Board since 1996.
