The Associate Arts, Culture and Heritage Minister Judith Tizard today announced a new appointment to the Board of Te Papa Tongarewa.

Dr John Henderson is Associate Professor in Political Science at the University of Canterbury, specialising in New Zealand and Pacific Island history and politics and international relations. He has strong academic experience and historical knowledge and is currently a member of the Pacific Cooperation Foundation Board.

Dr Henderson has been appointed for a three-year term from 15 September 2006.

The Minister says she is delighted to welcome Dr Henderson as a new Board member.

“This appointment will bring useful academic and historical experience to the Board table and will add a Pacific perspective to Board deliberations,” she said.
