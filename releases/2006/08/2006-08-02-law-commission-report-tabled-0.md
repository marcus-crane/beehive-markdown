Minister Responsible for the Law Commission Mark Burton welcomed the Law Commission report Access to Court Records, which was tabled in Parliament today.

"The Law Commission's report examines the complex issues relating to public access to court records and provides a comprehensive review of the rules governing access to court records across all jurisdictions," Mark Burton said.
