Legislation is necessary to validate expenses incurred in breach of section 4 of the Public Finance Act 1989.

The only legal remedy for such a breach is for the expenses to be validated by an Act of Parliament.

- A summary of Treasury advice to Finance Minister Dr Michael Cullen on why validating legislation is necessary is available [here](http://www.beehive.govt.nz/?q=node%2F27438)
- The Cabinet paper detailing that advice is available [here](http://www.beehive.govt.nz/?q=node%2F27439)
- The text of the Appropriation (Parliamentary Expenditure Validation) Bill currently being debated in the House can be viewed [here](http://www.parliament.nz/en-NZ/PB/Legislation/Bills/0/5/9/059c0a68bd9d4248b79cb71f439f3973.htm)
