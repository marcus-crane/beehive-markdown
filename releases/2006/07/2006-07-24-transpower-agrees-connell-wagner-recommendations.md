The state-owned transmission company Transpower has agreed to the recommendations in the [Connell Wagner report](http://www.med.govt.nz/templates/MultipageDocumentTOC____20815.aspx), as summarised by Energy Minister David Parker in a [letter to Transpower on 7 July 2006](/parker/parker-letter-transpower070706.pdf) (10Kb .pdf).

The company's detailed response, dated 21 July 2006, is attached in the clickable link at the head of this document (884Kb .pdf).
