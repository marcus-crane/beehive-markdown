Dargaville landowners are getting over $3,000 in grant funding to help protect to help preserve wildlife and natural habitat.

Labour MP Dover Samuels announced today that a grant of $3,100 will complement contributions from Northland Regional Council and QEII National Trust to buy plants for the Horrobin Wetland Planting project on covenanted property 20km east of Dargaville.

The local grants are part of a nationwide announcement by Environment Minister David Benson-Pope and Associate Conservation Minister Mahara Okeroa today that $1.3 million is being distributed to 104 projects across the country.
