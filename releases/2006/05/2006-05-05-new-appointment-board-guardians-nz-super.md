5 May 2006Media Statement  
Appointment to Board of Guardians of New Zealand Superannuation Fund.

Finance Minister Michael Cullen today announced that Mark Tume has been appointed to the Board of Guardians of New Zealand Superannuation.

â€œThe [Board of Guardians](http://www.nzsuperfund.co.nz/index.asp?pageID=2145832392) is responsible for setting the investment policies of the [New Zealand Superannuation Fund](http://www.nzsuperfund.co.nz/) and for overseeing its operations. I am confident that Mr Tume has the skills to contribute effectively to the Board, Dr Cullen said.

Mark Tume is the latest appointment, bringing the total number of board members to seven (the maximum).

Mr Tume was previously Head of Funds Management at BNZ. He is currently on the board of Transpower, Ngai Tahu holdings, and Lake Taupo Funding. He is also the CEO of Grosvenor Group which provides wealth management services to the financial planning industry.

His term runs until 31 March 2011.
