Internal Affairs Minister Rick Barker has advised that the reporting date for the Commission of Inquiry into Police Conduct has been extended from 3 March to 31 May 2006.

Given the volume of submissions received the Commission has requested and has been granted the extension.

For further information, please contact the Commission spokesperson, Robert Brewer 021 505 044.
