Attorney-General David Parker today announced that an appointment round for Queen’s Counsel will be held this year.

The appointments for this year’s round will be made on the same basis as previous years, in accordance with existing regulations.

Applicants should apply in writing to the Solicitor-General by 28 February 2006 and include the information required by the Practice Note dated 12 August 1991.

Applicants will be notified of the outcome by 15 May 2006.
