The appointment of Rick Ellis as CEO of TVNZ was welcomed by Broadcasting Minister Steve Maharey.

"Rick Ellis is well qualified for this role and brings a wealth of experience, both in the broadcasting sector and as a senior executive," Steve Maharey said.

"He is stepping into a company that is rating well, returning a good dividend and getting on with the job of implementing its charter.

"I'm looking forward to his leadership of TVNZ as it moves into a new era that will be characterised by the move to digital and increasing diversity in the media landscape."
