The Economic Development Ministry has appointed Connell Wagner to review Transpower's report on the Auckland outage, Energy Minister David Parker said today.

Connell Wagner were provided with a copy of the reports commissioned by Transpower on Friday, June 23. Consultants from Connell Wagner's Australian operation are expected in New Zealand today.

Mr Parker said the review would report back by July 6.
