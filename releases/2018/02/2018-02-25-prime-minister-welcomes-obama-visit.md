Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern today welcomed confirmation of the visit to New Zealand of Barack Obama, the 44th President of the United States.

“I look forward to welcoming Mr Obama to our country and anticipate meeting him once his full programme is finalised,” says Jacinda Ardern.

The former President will speak at an event hosted by the New Zealand-United States Council in Auckland on 22 March.
