Internal Affairs Minister Tracey Martin today announced the appointment of Paul Meredith as a member of the Guardians Kaitiaki of the Alexander Turnbull Library.

The Guardians Kaitiaki has a statutory role to provide the Minister with independent advice on key matters associated with the operation of the Alexander Turnbull Library.

Mr Meredith will join Lyn Provost as chair, and the other current members Dr Jock Phillips, Dr Sydney Shep, and Victoria Passau.

“The appointment expands the range of experience, skills and perspectives that will ensure the Guardians Kaitiaki has the expertise to continue providing high quality advice to me.

Mr Meredith will bring his experience in mātauranga Māori as well as further the Guardians Kaitiaki knowledge of information technology and management, research knowledge, and governance.

Mr Meredith’s three year term commenced from 20 November 2018.
